Event Details

Welcome to the OG Prompt Engineer challenge, where your coding skills will be pushed to the limit. Unlike traditional contests, you are free to use any AI model or LLM to assist you in the contest. The key is to implement logic so complex that even AI struggles to replicate it perfectly. Are you ready to take on the challenge and prove yourself?

Date & Time: March 19,2025 (1:30 - 4:20 PM)

Team Size: 1-3 members per team

Eligibility: Open to 1st, 2nd, and 3rd year students

Levels

Level 1 - JavaScript Trickery

Master the art of complex JavaScript patterns and implementations.

Level 2 - Complex Animations

Create stunning visual effects that push the boundaries of web animation.

Level 3 - Pixel-Perfect Cloning

Demonstrate your ability to recreate complex designs with perfect precision.

Submission Process

How to Submit

  • ⏱️
    Time Limit: Each level has a specific time limit. The timer will be shown during the contest.
  • 📝
    Required Files:
    • Your complete project folder with all source code

  • ✅
